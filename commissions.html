    Ordering Process



    1. Contact me and tell me what you'd like using the given template.
    2. Once I have confirmed the commission, please send me the payment through PayPal. If I do not receive the payment within a week, I will have assumed that you have cancelled the commission.
    3. I will begin working on the commission as soon as I receive the payment. I will send screenshots as I go along and keep in contact. (Let me know if you want any specific screenshots during this time).
    4. When I have finished, I will confirm if you want to make any small last minute revisions. I will send the final piece to you through Twitter, Instagram, or email; whichever one you prefer.
